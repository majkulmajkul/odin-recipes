"The Replicant" Cocktail

Ingridients

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1 oz blue curacao
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 1 dash bitters
  • 1 oz dry ice (optional)

Instructions

In a shaker, combine the gin, blue curacao, lemon juice, simple syrup, and bitters. Fill the shaker with ice and shake well to combine.

Pour the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.

If using dry ice, add a small piece to the glass. The dry ice will create a smokey effect, giving the cocktail a futuristic, otherworldly look.

Garnish with a lemon twist and serve immediately.

Enjoy your trip to the neon-lit streets of future Los Angeles!
