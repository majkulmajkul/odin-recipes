The Artificial Chicken Soup

Ingridients

  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced bok choy
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 cup sliced onions
  • 1 cup sliced scallions
  • 8 oz thin rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil over high heat.

Add the garlic, mushrooms, bok choy, bell peppers, carrots, celery, and onions to the pot.

Reduce the heat to medium and simmer for 10 minutes.

Add the rice noodles and simmer for an additional 5 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.

Stir in the soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, white pepper, and salt.

Serve the soup hot, garnished with the sliced scallions.

I hope you enjoy this dish inspired by the theme of Blade Runner!
