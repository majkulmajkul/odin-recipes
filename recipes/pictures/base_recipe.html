"The Replicant" Cocktail

Ingridients

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1 oz blue curacao
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 1 dash bitters
  • 1 oz dry ice (optional)

Instructions

Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook until browned on all sides.

Add the bell peppers, garlic, and green onions to the pan and cook until the vegetables are tender.

Add the cooked noodles to the pan, along with the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Toss to coat the noodles and vegetables evenly.

Serve hot, garnished with additional green onions if desired. Enjoy!
